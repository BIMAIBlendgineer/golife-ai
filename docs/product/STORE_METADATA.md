# GoLife AI Store Metadata

## Positioning

- Product name: `GoLife AI`
- Short tagline: `Three clearer decisions for real life, every day.`
- Core promise: `Capture life quickly, get three realistic missions, act locally, and learn what actually helps.`

## Short Description

`GoLife AI is a local-first personal operating system for daily decisions across tasks, habits, money, pantry, week planning, and anti-impulse buying.`

## Long Description

`GoLife AI turns scattered life signals into small decisions you can actually act on. Capture a sentence, confirm one or more structured items, and let the app turn your local LifeGraph into three realistic missions for today.`

`The product is built around a local-first flow: capture, mission, action, feedback. Tasks, habits, spending, pantry risks, weekly planning, and purchase intentions stay useful even when the network is unavailable. AI is used only when explicitly allowed by privacy settings and only through GoLife services.`

`GoLife AI is not another isolated productivity tool. It coordinates trade-offs across daily life and explains why something matters today, with visible evidence, uncertainty, and fast feedback.`
