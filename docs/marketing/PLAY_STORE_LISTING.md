## Full description

GoLife AI turns scattered daily signals into three realistic actions you can actually do today.

Capture one sentence. Confirm the useful pieces. Let the app build a local-first picture of tasks, habits, money pressure, pantry risk, weekly planning, and impulse-buy decisions. From there, GoLife AI helps you focus on three small missions with visible evidence and clear uncertainty.

What makes it different:

- local-first by default
- privacy controls before AI
- evidence-backed daily missions
- one place for tasks, habits, money, pantry, planning, and anti-impulse decisions
- export, delete, and AI-history controls built into settings

What you can do:

- capture freeform life updates
- keep a local LifeGraph of what matters
- review three daily missions
- give feedback so suggestions improve over time
- choose your language, theme, quiet hours, reminder frequency, units, region, AI detail level, and current plan state

Privacy posture:

- your personal graph stays local by default
- only allowed AI flows can send filtered data
- admin systems are metadata-only, not a full cloud copy of your life data

Important boundaries:

- not medical advice
- not legal advice
- not regulated financial advice
- not an enterprise identity or SSO product
