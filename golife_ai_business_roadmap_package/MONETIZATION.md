# GoLife AI Monetization

## Launch model

GoLife should launch with two plans only:

- Free
- Plus at `EUR 7.99 / month`

Do not launch with Pro, Family, Lifetime or credit packs. Extra pricing layers add complexity before the core loop is proven.

## Free plan

- unlimited manual capture
- unlimited local history
- up to `20` AI actions per month
- no advanced weekly AI review
- no heavy cross-domain synthesis

## Plus plan

- higher AI action cap
- full daily missions
- weekly review and cross-domain recommendations
- faster access to new agents
- export and trust features stay available, not paywalled

## Base planning assumptions

- AI cost per call: `EUR 0.005`
- Free AI calls per month: `12`
- Paid AI calls per month: `90`
- free-to-paid conversion: `4%`
- monthly churn: `7%`
- support cost per paid user: `EUR 0.80`
- shared infra per MAU: `EUR 0.15`

## Conclusion

- break-even ARPU floor in the base scenario is about `EUR 0.26 / MAU`
- `EUR 7.99` is materially safer than `EUR 5.99` while usage and retention are still uncertain
- free AI must stay capped until conversion is real

## First experiments

1. Test `12` vs `20` free AI actions per month.
2. Test paywall timing after first useful mission vs after first week.
3. Measure whether explanation quality improves conversion more than extra features.
4. Delay all multi-seat or family packaging until retention is healthy.
