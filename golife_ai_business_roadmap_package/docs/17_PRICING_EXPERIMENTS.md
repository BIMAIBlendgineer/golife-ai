# 17 - Pricing Experiments

## Experiment 1 - Free + Plus

Free:

- unlimited manual capture;
- 20 AI actions per month.

Plus:

- EUR 7.99 / month;
- higher AI usage;
- cross-domain daily missions;
- weekly planning.

Success thresholds:

- paid conversion >= 4%;
- AI cost <= 20% of paid revenue;
- D30 paid retention >= 85%.

## Experiment 2 - Trial

7-day full-feature trial.

Success thresholds:

- trial activation >= 40%;
- trial-to-paid >= 10%.

## Experiment 3 - Annual plan

- EUR 79 / year.

Success thresholds:

- annual share >= 25% of paid users;
- lower effective churn than monthly.

## Experiments deferred

Do not run at launch:

- credit packs;
- Family plan;
- Lifetime access.

They add packaging complexity before core willingness to pay is proven.
