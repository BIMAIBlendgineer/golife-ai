# 15 - Risk Register

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Early signal | Mitigation |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|
| Scope is too wide | High | High | onboarding feels like six apps | keep wedge on daily missions only |
| License contamination | Medium | High | copied code or shared assets appear in review | clean-room rebuild and legal audit before implementation |
| AI cost drifts up | High | Medium | free usage rises faster than conversion | hard caps, mock mode, cheaper models, monitor cost per MAU |
| Users do not capture enough data | High | High | low capture count in first 3 days | unify capture, reduce friction, text first |
| Recommendations feel generic | Medium | High | low usefulness marks or repeated missions | tighter context retrieval, evidence, feedback loop |
| Privacy trust is weak | High | High | users disable AI or ask about data sharing | domain permissions, visible trace, local-first messaging |
| UX feels confusing | Medium | High | weak activation, long time to first mission | Home Today as center, progressive disclosure |
| Beta retention is weak | Medium | High | D7 below target | narrow scope further before public launch |
| App store review risk | Low-Medium | Medium | warnings around claims or subscriptions | careful wording, disclaimers, safe defaults |
| Professional-advice liability | Medium | High | unsafe prompts or user complaints | safety refusal, confirmation gates, no advice claims |
