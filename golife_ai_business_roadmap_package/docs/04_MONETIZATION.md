# 04 - Monetization Strategy

## Principle

GoLife AI should monetize on utility, not on data extraction.

The user keeps their data.
The paid value is:

- better missions;
- higher AI limits;
- cross-domain coordination;
- weekly review and diagnostics.

## Recommended model

### Free

- unlimited manual capture;
- local-first usage;
- basic Today screen;
- limited AI: up to 20 AI actions per month;
- no advanced cross-domain review;
- no weekly AI planning.

### Plus - Recommended launch plan

**EUR 7.99 / month**

Includes:

- daily missions;
- task diagnosis;
- spending reflection;
- pantry plan;
- explanation and trace;
- higher monthly AI allowance;
- lightweight weekly planning.

### Later plans

Not for launch:

- Pro
- Family
- Lifetime

These add complexity before the core loop is validated.

## What should not be monetized

- selling personal data;
- opaque sponsored recommendations;
- financial targeting;
- privacy downgrade as a paywall.

## Paywall rule

Never lock user-owned data behind the paywall.

What can be limited:

- AI volume;
- advanced cross-domain synthesis;
- weekly reviews;
- deeper diagnostics.

## Cost-control rule

The MVP must remain useful even when AI is disabled.

That means:

- local capture always works;
- basic classification can be local or mocked;
- AI is an accelerator, not a hard dependency.
