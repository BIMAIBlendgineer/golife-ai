# Brand and Copy

## Brand personality

Calm, practical, direct.

## Avoid

- mystical productivity language;
- overpromising;
- guilt;
- financial miracle claims;
- therapy claims.

## Use

- small decisions;
- realistic day;
- save money;
- use what you already have;
- recover after missing a habit.

## Example UX copy

- “Today looks overloaded. I reduced your plan to 3 realistic missions.”
- “This is probably a microspend pattern, not a financial emergency.”
- “Use these two foods first before buying more.”
