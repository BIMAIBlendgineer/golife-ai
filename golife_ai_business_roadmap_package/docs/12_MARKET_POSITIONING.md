# 12 - Market Positioning

## Recommended category

Primary category:

**AI Daily Mission System**

Extended category:

- personal life operating system;
- AI planner;
- habit mission layer;
- frugal living assistant.

## Indirect competitors

- Todoist / TickTick / Any.do
- Habitica / Streaks
- YNAB / Wallet / Spendee
- pantry and meal-planning apps
- wardrobe apps
- Notion-based self-management systems
- ChatGPT manual workflows

## Differential message

Competitors organize information.
GoLife should help users decide what to do today.

## Taglines

1. "Your day, turned into missions."
2. "Less chaos. Better small decisions."
3. "Tasks, money, food and habits in one AI daily plan."
4. "Stop managing apps. Start managing life."
5. "The AI that connects your real life."

## Spanish positioning

1. "Tu dia convertido en misiones."
2. "Menos caos, mejores decisiones pequenas."
3. "Tareas, dinero, comida y habitos en un solo plan diario."
4. "Deja de administrar apps. Organiza tu vida real."

## Final recommended positioning

GoLife AI should first be positioned as:

> the app that gives you 3 realistic missions every morning.

It should not launch positioned as:

- a full productivity suite;
- a finance tool;
- a family hub;
- a wardrobe platform;
- a meal planner.
